Year 4 Project: Project based around the General Data Protection Regulation

I completed a project during my 4th year in college, which was based around the incoming General Data Protection Regulation. I started this project by researching the area of the General Data Protection Regulation in detail. I then progressed to focus on the auditing aspect of the General Data Protection Regulation from an organizational perspective. The aim of my project was to help and advise organizations who use a network attached storage solution, on how best to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. This was achieved through the use of monitoring tools and creating various bash scripts to extract the information organizations will need to be able to prove compliance with the regulation. I thoroughly enjoyed completing a project in this area and felt I gained key skills along the way, such as upskilling in the area of bash scripting.
